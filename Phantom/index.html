Astrophysical Fluids and Plasma Astrophysics Group

Welcome to our webpage. We study astrophysical fluids and plasmas at the International Centre for Theoretical Sciences of the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research. The fundamental themes of research we pursue include magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) turbulence, astrophysical dynamos and magnetic reconnection. We apply lessons from these to understand the

  • generation and sustenence of stellar, interstellar and inter-galactic magnetic fields;
  • accretion disks dynamics and associated jets;
  • cosmic magnetic field evolution;
  • non-thermal emmision from astrophysical sources;
